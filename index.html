Simple Samon Supper

1. Preheat the oven to gas mark 7, 220ºC , fan 200ºC. Cook the potatoes in a pan of boiling water for 20-25 mins, until tender. Drain and return to the pan. Using the back of a fork lightly crush the potatoes.

2. Meanwhile, rub 1tbsp of the oil over the salmon fillets, put on a baking sheet llined with foil and season. Roast in the oven for 8-10 mins, until cooked through.

3. Heat a non-stick frying pan over a high heat. Add the lardons and stir-fry for 4-5 mins, until crisp. Add the potatoes and fry for 1 min to color. Mix in the peas and cook, stirring occasionally, for 1-2 mins until defrosted and heated thrugh.

4. Heat the remaining oil in a seperate fring pan over a medium heat. Add the spring onions and cook, stirring occasionally, for 2-3 mins to soften. Mix in the creme fraiche, mustard, dill and capers, adding a dash of water if the sauce seems a little thick. Season and bring to the boil then remove from the heat.

5. To serve, divide the salmon and potatoes between 4 plates and spoon over the sauce. Garnish with a little chopped dill and lemon wedges on the side.

    Ingredients

  • 500g new potatoes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 wild sockeye salmon fillets
  • 60g smoked bacon lardons
  • 200g frozen peas
  • 4 spring onions
  • 200ml creme fraiche
  • ½ tsp English mustard
  • 1 tbsp dill, frinely chopped,
    plus extra to garnish
  • 1 tbsp drained capers, roughly
    chopped
  • ½ lemon, cut into wedges to serve
